Submission Procedure

Students take, in succession, three mathematics courses, each compressed into three weeks. While these courses will be demanding, program participants are compensated by their small class size and highly focused approach. In addition to the math sequence, program participants take a French language course, which runs at a normal pace through the quarter, a course designed to help students connect with French (and Parisian) culture.

Who We Are

Academus Journal is a student-run, peer-reviewed publication showcasing the best high-school research in the humanities and social sciences.

What We Offer

  • Rigorous Peer Review: Student editors evaluate every submission for clarity, originality, and scholarly rigor.
  • Open Access: All articles are freely available online, promoting widespread dissemination and impact.
  • Persistent Identifiers: Each issue and article is assigned ISSNs and DOIs for academic discoverability.
  • Digital Platform: Responsive design with powerful search and filter tools to help readers find exactly what they need.
  • Our goal is to empower tomorrow’s scholars by providing a professional, supportive publishing environment—no paywalls, no barriers.
